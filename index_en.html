3GLOW Controller

3GLOW device lets you control your LED lighting via IOS or Android.

Features

Lighting is about more than just illuminating the dark. With 3GLOW application, it's easy to change the color of your lighting and enlivening the environment with newly added features. You can automatically control the LED lighting by criteria: speed, acceleration, altitude, rotation, camera, music and more.

  • 16 Million Color16 Million vibrant colors via a single tap. High resolution color palette with adjustable brightness and saturation.
  • Favourite ColorsElegant colors list for easy access
  • MusicSync lights with music rythm. Supports both microphone and any song in your phone with 15 different music profiles
  • CameraRealtime camera function enables capturing any color around you. Whether it's a stunning new shirt or a beautiful sunflower, just point your phone's camera to it.
  • RotationChange color by rotating your mobile device
  • AltitudeChange color with altitude. Long tap allows you to adjust the scale.
  • AccelerationSync light with speed, acceleration determines the color.
  • MoodTake advantage of biological benefits of light; Reading, Relax, Concentrate, Energize, Ambition, Trust, Creativity..
  • PalettePick a photo to use as your palette. Then simply drag the color picker across the image and your lighting will match the color instantly
  • Strobe LightsStrobe light effects with adjustable color and speed
  • Light EffectsImpressive special light effects enable more enjoy (Rainbow, Party, Disco, WallWasher, Aurora, Candle, Torch...)
  • TimerTimer feature automatically turns on/off the lights after specified time
  • Holiday ModeSimulates human presence by randomly turning on/off the lights. If you’re not home, turning the lights on in the evenings can make it seem like you’re still there.
  • Double ConnectionYou can connect the 3GLOW's WiFi network or 3GLOW can connect to your home WiFi or both!
  • EnglishEnglish language support
  • SafeStrong password protection for access and encrypted communication
  • Multiple ControlAutomatically recognition all the 3GLOW devices around. Multiple connection feature.
